# Insights from Factory Machine Downtime Analysis

## 1. Factory with Highest Downtime
The analysis of telemetry data shows that one factory experienced significantly higher downtime compared to the others. This indicates possible operational or maintenance challenges at that location.

## 2. Machine Types Causing Maximum Failures
Some machine types showed higher unhealthy status frequency than others. These machines may require more frequent maintenance or inspection.

## 3. Downtime Pattern
Downtime occurred due to repeated unhealthy machine status messages, each representing approximately 10 minutes of potential downtime.

## 4. Operational Implications
High downtime can affect production efficiency and operational costs. Identifying the machines responsible helps prioritize maintenance efforts.

## 5. Recommendation
Factories should focus on preventive maintenance for machines that show frequent unhealthy status to reduce production downtime and improve reliability.
